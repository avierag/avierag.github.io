Our Excursions

One Day Habana

Through this excursion the clients could explore the most interesting sites of the city, and will get in touch with its real life and traditions.

The one day excursion to Habana city starts with the picking up at the hotel at 8 o´clock am and will be finished with the return to the hotel at around 6 pm. The drive to Habana from Varadero takes approximately two hours and forty minutes.

On the way to Habana there is a stopover at a view point next to the highest bridge in Cuba, to enjoy the landscape of a splendid valley and the Atlantic Ocean, the clients may taste at this glamorous place the nicest pinacolada ever for a price of 5 USD/Euros.

Once in the city but before getting into it, a stopover, next to the Morro Castle, declared a world heritage by UNESCO, to view an unforgettable panorama of the city, just few meters further another stopover this time at the statue of the Christ of Habana, created by the Cuban artist Rita Longa, this is the highest view point of the city, the clients could also visit the surroundings of the Cabaña fortress where there is a tobacco shop and the longest cigar in the world at display, there is a good opportunity to buy cigars and rum, two of the typical Cuban products.

After driving through the tunnel under the bay the arrival to the city center to start the walking trip right from the majestic capitol building to the historical old Habana, visiting the four most attractive and oldest city squares, this is a unique opportunity to enjoy the ambience of narrow streets and to view a breathtaking scenery.

After the walking tour the clients might have one hour for lunch time, the local guide may recommend a good privately owned restaurant.

Last but not least a driving tour through the modern Havana with a stopover at the revolution square. The tour will be ending in Habana at the 16 hours.

Recommendations: to bring proper clothing and shoes to walk on the cobble stones streets of the old city, solar protector and water.

For the reconfirmation of the tour it’s compelling to know the name of the hotel, room number, and a photo of the clients.

Tour price:

  • Up to 4 persons 180 USD/Euros.
  • From 5 to 6 persons 250 USD/Euros.
  • From 7 to 9 persons 360 USD/Euros.

Note: the price only includes transportation and guiding service.

Habana Over Night

The two days tour to Havana starts when the clients are picked up at the hotel at 9:30 am, the driving lasts approximately two hours and forty minutes including a stopover for photos.

The excursion includes the visit to the most interesting sites of the city and the possibility to get in touch with the Cuban reality, its history and culture, the lodging can be arranged in privately owned houses in the city center where the clients could hang around on their own going to bars and places of the city at night time, or make use of the transportation provided to go to Tropicana night club, or to Buena vista social band show, the transportation isn´t included in the price of the tour, the clients should be properly dressed to visit this places mentioned and to bring a photocopy of the passport.

During the first day the guiding tour includes the visit of the old Havana, starting the walking trip at majestic Capitol building with time at disposal for photos, the walking tour includes the visit to the four historical city squares and the famous bars frequented by the American writer Ernest Hemingway, as well as the oldest fortress in the city.

The clients will have one hour time for lunch in any of the privately owned restaurants suggested by the tour guide The second day includes the visit to some interesting places in the city such as the handcraft market, the surroundings of La Cabaña and Morro castles, the tobacco shop to see the longest cigar in the world, and time for shopping, at the end the visit to the monumental statue of the Christ of Habana, from where the clients can observe the city at all its large.

Recommendations: to bring proper clothing and shoes for the walking on the cobble stone streets of the city, solar protector and water.

For the reconfirmation of the tour it´s compelling to know the name of the hotel, room number, and a photo of the clients Price of the excursion.

Tour price:

  • Up to four persons 200 USD/Euros per day.
  • From 5 to 6 persons 300 USD/Euros per day.

Note: the price only includes transportation and guiding service.

Tour To The Caribbean

On this Caribbean day tour the clients get much in touch with Cuban flora and fauna since the clients may visit the largest crocodile breeding farm of the island, besides swimming and scuba diving in the Caribbean sea, also, as we drive in land and all across the island from north to south the clients can see in the way little communities and crops, the drive takes around two and a half hour, the pick up at the hotel takes place at the 8 hours.

First stop takes place at the crocodile breeding farm, where crocodiles are raised and protected to avoid its extinction, time at disposal to visit the breeding farm.

Lunch isn’t included but the guide will recommend a very nice restaurant with very exotic dishes at a price which do not exceed the 20 USD/Euros per person, the lunch includes, besides the main course, rice, vegetables and one drink.

After lunch, time for swimming at Caribbean Sea where the water is warmer compare to the Atlantic in Varadero, It’s recommended to bring pieces of bread to feed the little fish that will come close ashore by hundreds; There is an option for scuba diving at your disposal the price is of 25 USD/Euros for 40 minutes, the beginners should pay 10 additional Euros for specialized instructions that cannot be skipped, and it’s a very easy scuba diving at a depth of 10 to 15 meters.

A visit to a natural well, known as the fish cave is included, with time for swimming in its cool salty water. As an optional activity the clients may visit the hummingbird house (the smallest bird in the world) for only 1 USD/Euros.

Return to Varadero at the 16 hours.

Tour price:

  • from 2 to 4 persons 180 USD/Euros.
  • from 5 to 7 persons 250 USD/Euros.
  • from 8 to 10 persons 360 USD/Euros.

Note: the price only includes transportation and guiding service.

Soroa Orchid Park, Viñales Valley

Pick up time at the lobby of the hotel at the 6 hours, the duration of the driving trip is of approximately 4 hours, with a stopover in the way.

Half way from the last stop, one of the highlights of this excursion is the visited, Soroa Orchid sanctuary, a park where the client can observe a very large variety of orchids and other exotic plants, this paradisiacal site is located on the Rosario mountain range, in the same place the clients can visit the Soroa look out and the rainbow water fall, in this last site the clients could swim in the fresh water of the river at a price of 5 USD/Euros.

The last stop takes place at viñales valley, the well called the Cuban garden, with an impressive view of Los Organos mountain range which surrounds the valley and the reason why this place is extraordinarily beautiful and mysterious, there is a visit to a cave being inhabited by aboriginals back in times, and a boat trip along the underground river inside the cave, there is also a visit to a tobacco plantation where the best tobacco in the world is cultivated, the clients will also learn about process of rolling cigars and will even try one if desired, with shopping opportunity.

Lunch in a local restaurant with traditional Cuban dishes at a price of 15 USD/Euros per person Return to the hotel at approximately the 21 hours.

Two Cities One Beach

Pick up time at the 6 hours at the hotel lobby and it’s over with the arrival to the hotel at the 20 hours. Following this order the visit of Cienfuegos city, beach time at ancon peninsula, and the visit to Trinidad city.

Cienfuegos also called the ¨Perl of the south¨ is located on the south coasts with a very strong French influence in its culture and architecture, the city tour starts at its main avenue and continue to the center park, in all this area the most important buildings and attractions of the city could be observed, like the statue of the famous Cuban musician Beny More, known as the king of the salsa, the majestic former Governor palace, the arc of triumph, Tomas Terry Theater, and the Cathedral of the immaculate conception, among others, after the walking , the tour continues by car all along the main avenue in direction to punta gorda peninsula to enjoy the view of the bay and the former homes of the rich French people that lived in the city centuries ago, like the famous Valle Palace.

Next stop at Ancon beach in the surroundings of Trinidad city and 71 kilometers away from Cienfuegos, one of the most beautiful beaches of the Caribbean coasts, beach time at disposal, because of the lack of public showers, the clients might use their own transportation for changing clothes.

For lunch its recommended a buffet restaurant near the beach on the way to Trinidad city for a price of 15 Euros, drinks not included.

After the relaxing swimming and lunch time, the excursion continues to Trinidad, considered by foreigners the second most popular city in Cuba after Havana, the historical center of Trinidad was declared by UNESCO a world heritage in 1982, and it keeps its original aspect, people generally say that it’s a city where the time stopped. Walking through the cobble stones of the city is like going back in time, the most important site of the city is the major square ¨plaza Mayor¨ and the buildings that surround the square, the former homes of counts and marquises. After the walking tour in Trinidad, return to varadero.

Nicho Water Fall, Trinidad and Cienfuegos

Visiting the national park ¨EL NICHO¨ is an incredible adventure, where clients can swim and shower with the waters of a magnificent water fall surrounded by exuberant vegetation, and the visit of Trinidad and Cienfuegos to get in touch with the history and the culture of two very different cities.

Pick up time at 05:30 hours at the hotel lobby, the drive to first stop takes approximately 4 hours, once in the place in order to reach the water fall, there is a walking trip along a path on the mountain, for that reason the clients must bring proper clothing and shoes, the temperature of the water is very fresh in summer and cool in winter, the visit to the place takes around 2 hours.

For lunch it’s recommended a buffet restaurant in the way to Trinidad at a cost of 15 USD/Euros per persons, drinks not included in the price.

Continuation to Trinidad city for a walking tour which lasts 2 hours, walking on the cobble stones streets around the historical center is like going back in time, the most important site of the city is the major square ¨plaza Mayor¨ and the buildings that surround the square, the former homes of counts and marquises, nowadays museums and cultural projects that could be visited by the clients.

On the way back in direction to Cienfuegos city there is short stop to by fresh local fruits.

Matanzas City, Yumury Valley

Pick up time at the hotel lobby at 9 am, the most important touristic places around Matanzas city will be visited on this tour; this beautiful city is known as the ¨Athens of Cuba¨ due to its great economic and cultural development during the XIX century.

The first place to be visited will be Mapita beach where there is an option for scuba diving accompanied by a specialized guide at a price 30 USD/Euros.

Later there is time for swimming in a natural cave with crystalline cool water, at a price of 5 USD/Euros Next stop will be at Canimar River where the clients have the possibility to drive themselves speeding boats along the river, driving license isn’t required, the boats have a capacity for 4 persons, and the cost is 35 USD/Euros per boat, along the river a tropical humid wood can be closely observed.

Last but not least Matanzas city tour, most of it by walking to get in touch with the architecture, history and culture of the city, after the walking tour and by car there is a visit to the highest view point in the city from where the clients can observe the city and the second most beautiful valley in Cuba.

If by any chance the scuba diving isn’t possible to be accomplished, then the Bellamar cave will be visited, this place is considered the oldest touristic site of the island of Cuba, it’s being opened to public for 150 years, proper clothing and shoes recommended, the relative humidity is of almost 100 % and it might be slippery somewhere, the price to visit the cave is of 5 USD/Euros per person and 5 USD/Euros for the right to bring the photo camera.

Lunch isn’t included but the tour guide may suggest a good restaurant in the city.

Return to the hotel at around 3:00 pm.

For the reconfirmation of the tour it´s compelling to know the name of the hotel, room number, and a photo of the clients.
