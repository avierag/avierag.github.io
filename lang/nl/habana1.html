One Day Habana

Through this excursion the clients could explore the most interesting sites of the city, and will get in touch with its real life and traditions.

The one day excursion to Habana city starts with the picking up at the hotel at 8 o´clock am and will be finished with the return to the hotel at around 6 pm. The drive to Habana from Varadero takes approximately two hours and forty minutes.

On the way to Habana there is a stopover at a view point next to the highest bridge in Cuba, to enjoy the landscape of a splendid valley and the Atlantic Ocean, the clients may taste at this glamorous place the nicest pinacolada ever for a price of 5 USD/Euros.

Once in the city but before getting into it, a stopover, next to the Morro Castle, declared a world heritage by UNESCO, to view an unforgettable panorama of the city, just few meters further another stopover this time at the statue of the Christ of Habana, created by the Cuban artist Rita Longa, this is the highest view point of the city, the clients could also visit the surroundings of the Cabaña fortress where there is a tobacco shop and the longest cigar in the world at display, there is a good opportunity to buy cigars and rum, two of the typical Cuban products.

After driving through the tunnel under the bay the arrival to the city center to start the walking trip right from the majestic capitol building to the historical old Habana, visiting the four most attractive and oldest city squares, this is a unique opportunity to enjoy the ambience of narrow streets and to view a breathtaking scenery.

After the walking tour the clients might have one hour for lunch time, the local guide may recommend a good privately owned restaurant.

Last but not least a driving tour through the modern Havana with a stopover at the revolution square. The tour will be ending in Habana at the 16 hours.

Recommendations: to bring proper clothing and shoes to walk on the cobble stones streets of the old city, solar protector and water.

For the reconfirmation of the tour it’s compelling to know the name of the hotel, room number, and a photo of the clients.

Tour price:

  • Up to 4 persons 180 USD/Euros.
  • From 5 to 6 persons 250 USD/Euros.
  • From 7 to 9 persons 360 USD/Euros.

Note: the price only includes transportation and guiding service.
