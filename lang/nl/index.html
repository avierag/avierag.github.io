About us

Welcome to Unique Cuban Trip

We offer a great deal of travel packages to explore the diversity and charming of this Caribbean island. Since the vibrant Havana city with its lively night life and beautiful colonial architecture to the white sands and crystal waters of Varadero and Trinidad, These travel packages also provide personalized services to please the necessities and preferences of each client, from lodging in hotels and privately owned residencies to local tour guides and nongovernmental transportation.

Explore the amazing Cuban culture, music, history and nature, provided by a local travel agency and bring home the memories of an unforgettable vacation.

Travel Guides

We take care of accompanying and assisting tourists during their visit to a tourist destination. We have in-depth knowledge of the destination and its goal is to provide information and guidance to visitors so that they can enjoy their experience to the fullest.
Provide detailed information on the tourist, historical and cultural attractions of the destination.
Accompany the group of tourists during visits to places of interest and offer enriching explanations about them.
Coordinate activities and transportation for the tourist group.
Solve problems and unforeseen situations that may arise during the trip.
Provide recommendations on restaurants, accommodations and other activities in the destination.
