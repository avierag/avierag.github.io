Our Excursions

Classic Havana (two days)

A two-day tour with hotel pickup at 9:30am. The duration of the trip will be approximately 2:30h with an intermediate stop during the tour.

This is a tour that will take you to the most iconic and beautiful places in the city with time and patience. They will be able to see the Cuban reality, part of its culture and customs. They can stay in a rented house in the very historic center of the city, and at night there will be other activities that they can enjoy.

During the first day the most common tourist spots of the city will be visited and during the second day other places of interest such as the Great Havana Souvenir Market.

The price of the excursion does not include breakfast, lunch or dinner, only accommodation and in case you want to eat, the guide will be able to suggest the place to do it or the client will be able to do it on their own.

At night you can visit the Tropicana Cabaret in Havana, another option could be the "Buena Vista Social Club" show or if the client prefers, they can visit the different bars or other places that be of your interest without the presence of the guide.

It is recommended to wear appropriate clothing to walk through the cobbled streets of Havana, plenty of sunscreen and water.

If you want to go to a cabaret at night, you must wear suitable and/or appropriate clothing for these places, as well as have your passport and/or a photocopy of it.

Round trip transfers to the different places are not included in the price of the tour.

In case of confirmation for the tour you need to know the name of the hotel, room number and photos of the clients.

The price is 200 USD/Euros per day.

The price includes only transportation and guide services.
