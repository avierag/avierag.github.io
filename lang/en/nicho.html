El Nicho (Waterfalls), Trinidad and Cienfuegos

Visit to El Nicho National Park, where you can see its waterfalls and swim in its waters. Dinner in the City of Trinidad. Cienfuegos city.

Departure at 5:30 am from the hotel with a journey of approximately 4 hours to reach the waterfalls.

In the waterfalls you can go up the mountain path; therefore, it is necessary to wear appropriate footwear and clothing for this activity. The water temperature is quite cool in summer and somewhat cold in winter. This visit lasts approximately 2 hours.

For lunch is provided in the restaurant of local cuisine. (15 euros). Lunch is 1h. Buffet restaurant near Trinidad is recommended... (although the journey is long, it is better to eat a lot for lunch). Drinks are not included.

They will continue the trip to the city of Trinidad where the visit will last approximately 2 hours. You can enjoy a walking tour of the historic center, where you can find the Plaza Mayor, the Church of the Holy Trinity, the House of the Sánchez Isnaga, the Cantero Palace, the Museum of Romance, the House of the Conspirators, the Museum of the Anti-Communist Revolt, the Canchancara café, the House of Music and the stone staircase are located for concerts and dances. Duration of the tour: 1.30 h with an optional stop to buy fruit.

They will continue the trip to the city of Cienfuegos where the visit will last approximately 1.30h.

They will start with a walk through the center: Boulevard del Prado, the pedestrian street "El Bulevar", the central square of José Martí, there we will see the Thomas Terry Theater, the Palace of Mr. Ferrer, the Cathedral of the Immaculate Conception, the San Lorenzo College, a prestigious modern area "Punta Gorda" with a long promenade and a yacht club, the Palacio del Valle is a beautiful Moorish style mansion.

In case of confirmation for the tour you need to know the name of the hotel, room number and customer photos

The price of the tour :

  • for up to 4 people is 480 USD/Euros.
  • for up to 6 people is 600 USD/Euros.
  • for up to 9 people is 800 USD/Euros.

The price includes only transportation and guide services.
