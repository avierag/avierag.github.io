Our Excursions

Classic Havana (one day)

They will be able to visit some of the places of interest in the capital of Cuba, as well as the reality of this town and its customs.

A 1-day tour with hotel pickup at 8:00 am and ending in Havana at 4:00 pm. The duration of the trip will be approximately 2:40h.

On the way, a stop is made at the highest bridge in Cuba (Bacunayagua, 112 m) overlooking the Yumurí Valley and the Atlantic Ocean. The client may opt for a richer pina colada from Cuba 5 USD/Euros.

You will be able to make a stop near the Morro Fortress, from where an impressive panoramic view of Havana opens up. The Morro Fortress was declared a World Heritage Site by UNESCO and is one of the main architectural monuments of our capital (admission is paid and optional).

We continue with a visit to the Cristo de La Habana monument where the highest viewpoint in the city is located and a beautiful sculpture created in 1958 by the great Cuban sculptor Rita Longa. You will be able to visit the exterior areas of the great La Cabaña fortress, where there is a store with the largest tobacco in the world. If you want to buy this incomparable typical Cuban product you can do it; besides others.

Next, a walking tour for almost two hours, from the Capitol to the historic center of the city. You will see the main attractions of this place; such as: Plaza San Francisco de Asís, Plaza de la Catedral, Plaza de Armas (the place of official foundation of Havana in 1519), Plaza Vieja, the Palace of the Spanish Governors, the first fortress of Havana (Castillo de la Real Fuerza), walk along Obispo street (the busiest pedestrian street in the city), among other places further.

If the client wishes to have lunch, they will have an hour to do so in some of the private restaurants that the guide could suggest.

Finally, you will visit the Plaza de la Revolución and take a walk through the center of Havana, where the Capitol is located, the place where the meetings of the Cuban Congress used to be, the Gran Teatro (Gran Teatro de La Habana) , the Floridita café, about which Hemingway wrote and where celebrities popularized the Cuban daiquiri cocktail, the Museum of Fine Arts, Boulevard del Prado (Havana's main street), the Museum of the Revolution (former Presidential Palace), etc.

We leave Havana at 16:00.

  • for up to 4 people is 180 USD/Euros.
  • for 5 to 6 people is 250 USD/Euros.
  • for 7 to 9 people is 360 USD/Euros.

The price includes only transportation and guide services.

Classic Havana (two days)

A two-day tour with hotel pickup at 9:30am. The duration of the trip will be approximately 2:30h with an intermediate stop during the tour.

This is a tour that will take you to the most iconic and beautiful places in the city with time and patience. They will be able to see the Cuban reality, part of its culture and customs. They can stay in a rented house in the very historic center of the city, and at night there will be other activities that they can enjoy.

During the first day the most common tourist spots of the city will be visited and during the second day other places of interest such as the Great Havana Souvenir Market.

The price of the excursion does not include breakfast, lunch or dinner, only accommodation and in case you want to eat, the guide will be able to suggest the place to do it or the client will be able to do it on their own.

At night you can visit the Tropicana Cabaret in Havana, another option could be the "Buena Vista Social Club" show or if the client prefers, they can visit the different bars or other places that be of your interest without the presence of the guide.

It is recommended to wear appropriate clothing to walk through the cobbled streets of Havana, plenty of sunscreen and water.

If you want to go to a cabaret at night, you must wear suitable and/or appropriate clothing for these places, as well as have your passport and/or a photocopy of it.

Round trip transfers to the different places are not included in the price of the tour.

Caribbean Sea (Bay of Pigs)

During this excursion you will visit the interior of Cuba where you will be able to become familiar with the Cuban flora and fauna, you will be able to visit a crocodile farm, you will also be able to swim and/or dive in the Caribbean Sea.

They will cross the island from north to south where the trip lasts about 2:30 hours with departure from the hotel at 8 am.

On the way they will pass through the cities of Santa Marta and Cárdenas, the city of Jovellanos, where many descendants of African slaves live, the cooperative named after Vladimir Ilyich Lenin, the town of Australia, sugar cane plantations, bananas, citrus and other tropical crops.

First stop at a tourist mini-farm with typical Cuban plants and animals. They will be able to buy a delicious cane juice, a "Piña Colada" or other drinks, as well as see the largest crocodile farm in Cuba.

Lunch is not included and it will cost three types of meat, they can be: crocodile, crab, lobster, shrimp, fish, venison, snails, jutía or squid - €15-20 per person and the price also includes a side dish of rice with vegetables, salad and drink.

The trip will continue with a visit to the Caribbean Sea where the temperature of this sea is undoubtedly warmer than that of Varadero and with calmer waters. You can do a dive where it is advisable to bring loaves from the hotel to feed fish in the sea, as well as for turtles, iguanas, jutias, catfish in the river.

Diving will cost €25 for 40 minutes for those who have already dived. Beginners will have to pay an additional €10 for an introductory lesson. There is a very easy dive, they dive to a depth of 25-30 meters, there are no currents, they can take you to a sunken ship.

They will be able to visit cenotes or sinkholes as there are several of them in the jungle of the Bay of Pigs region. These have fresh and clear water where you can swim and/or enjoy a beautiful view. If the client wishes, they can also go to the Casa del Colibrí and see different types of birds.

Later you can swim on the sandy beach in the town of Playa Larga .

Return to Varadero at 4:00 p.m.

Soroa and Viñales Valley

The tour begins with hotel pickup at 6:00 am. The duration of the trip will be approximately 4 hours with an intermediate stop during the tour.

You will visit the Soroa viewpoint, which offers a panoramic view of the Sierra de Soroa and also the El Salto del Arco Iris waterfall where you can bathe.

Then continue the trip to the Valley of Viñales, this tour will be approximately 1:30 h

The Valley of Viñales is rightly called the "Garden of Cuba". The impressive view of the Sierra de Los Órganos mountains that surround the valley makes this place incredibly beautiful and mysterious. They will visit a cave that was inhabited by Cuban aborigines and take a boat ride through the underground river.

Next, you will visit a tobacco plantation where the most prestigious tobacco leaves in the world are grown. They will have the opportunity to familiarize us with the process and the elaboration of this product, as well as its tasting and also buy other products.

Trinidad, Ancon Beach and Cienfuegos

This excursion begins with the pick up at the hotel at 6 am and will end after 8 pm with the arrival in Varadero. The city of Cienfuegos will be visited, then Ancon Beach and they will end up visiting the city of Trinidad.

Cienfuegos, also called the "Pearl of the South" is located on the southern coast of Cuba and is known for its French influence in its architecture and culture. The city's historic center, known as "Punta Gorda," is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and features impressive examples of neoclassical architecture.

Most of the attractions of Cienfuegos are concentrated around its main artery, Avenida Prado. The tour with a Russian-speaking guide will start from José Martí Square, around which are some of the most famous sights: the Arc de Triomphe, the Government Palace, the Presidential Palace, the Thomas Terry Theater and the Cathedral of the Immaculate Conception.

After seeing these sites, you will head south on Avenida Prado and the Malecón towards the Bay of Cienfuegos. The most famous sight on the route is the Valle Palace, but you will also see other historical sites. Your visit will end in the Punta Gorda area, which used to be home to wealthy Frenchmen.

They will continue with the visit to Playa Ancón, considered one of the beautiful beaches of the southern Caribbean coast, where hotels of well-known international chains were built a long time ago. From here the pirates carried out their attacks on Trinidad and for this reason, the waters near Ancon are considered one of the main "cemeteries of sunken ships" near Cuba.

If clients wish, they will have beach time in the Caribbean Sea. Like most public beaches in Cuba, Ancon is not equipped with changing rooms and showers (in extreme cases, you can change on our transport).

After relaxing on Ancon beach, the tour continues to the city of Trinidad, the second most popular city among travelers after Havana. The colonial zone of Trinidad is included in the UNESCO World Heritage List and its historic center perfectly preserves its original appearance. The careful storage of the riches of the past was only possible thanks to the inhabitants of the city, who from generation to generation transmit love and care to every corner of the city.

The central nucleus of Trinidad is the main square (Plaza Mayor), around which important historical buildings are located, including the Romantic Museum and the Church of the Holy Trinity. Walking through Trinidad makes you feel as if you have been transported back to colonial times. At the end of your guided tour of Trinidad, you will return to Varadero.

Matanzas City - Yumuri

This excursion begins with the pick up at the Varadero beach at 9 am .

You will visit the most interesting tourist places of this beautiful city, well called "The Athens of Cuba" for its great cultural development in the 19th century. The first place to be visited will be the "Mapita" beach where you can go diving with a specialized guide.

This place is located approximately 15 km from Varadero and the price of the service (diving and guide) costs 30 USD/euros.

Afterwards, they will be able to bathe again in the natural cenote of crystalline and colder waters of the cave of Saturno.

Next, clients will have the possibility of driving a small motor boat through the most important river in the province, the Canimar river.

They will be able to appreciate a significant representation of the tropical humid forest, habitat of endemic animals and settlement of the first inhabitants of the island.

Finally, the historic center of the provincial capital of the province of Matanzas will be visited; the Matanzas city. You will visit on foot the most emblematic places of this beautiful city, the cradle of important poets and writers during the 19th century.

It also includes a visit to the most important viewpoint in the city, where due to its privileged position you will be able to observe the great extension of the second most important valley of Cuba "Yumurí" and of the city.

If, due to weather conditions or the clients' decision, diving cannot be carried out at Mapita beach, then the Bellamar cave will be visited. This place is well known and popular, it was the first historic center created in Cuba and it has been in operation for more than 150 years. Appropriate clothing is recommended for this place. relative humidity; almost 100% and very slippery.

Admission is 5 USD/Euros per person and cameras are another 5 USD/Euros.

Lunch is not included. If they wish, they can agree with the guide to reserve a restaurant in the very historic center of the city.
